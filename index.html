About Me

Marcus McBride here. I'm a recent graduate of the University Of California, Irvine's full stack coding bootcamp program. I currently reside and work in Orange County, California.

Over the years I've worked as a contract-based producer, programmer and project manager within the broadcast industry as well as a mobile DJ for Full Tilt Media LLC, my own media contracting company.

I have over two decades worth of experience in this arena and have worked for a number of media companies including Clear Channel/iHeartRadio, JamTraxx Media and XM Satellite Radio. I've primarily been responsible for producing syndicated broadcast content for these platforms as well as their affiliate partners. I thrive in a studio environment but work as equally well on-location as needed.

While the broadcast industry has been good to me over the years I am currently seeking my next digital challenge. You can view some of my recent development work on the portfolio page of this app. I'm open to new and challenging opportunities within the coding and development realm. Please feel free to reach out to me with any questions, feedback or inquiries.
